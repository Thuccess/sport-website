About Sport Website FC

Proudly representing South Sudan on the football stage. Learn about our mission, values, and journey.

Our History

Founded in 2013, Sport Website Football Club has grown into one of the most celebrated clubs in South Sudan. With passion, perseverance, and dedication, we have climbed the ranks of national competitions and brought pride to our fans.

Our Mission

To inspire and develop football talent while fostering community pride and sportsmanship.

Our Vision

To be a leading football club in East Africa, known for excellence, integrity, and passion for the game.

Our Values

Dedication, teamwork, respect, and commitment to our fans and community.
